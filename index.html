    2023 International Symposium on Microphysiological Systems （MPS 2023）and the 6th Academic Conference and Industrial Investment Forum on Organoid and Organ-on-Chip will be held in Nanjing, China from November 11th to 12th, 2023.

    The conference is jointly organized by Southeast University and Columbia University. Esteemed experts, scholars, and entrepreneurs engaged in research and application in the fields of organ-on-chip, organoid, tissue engineering, biomedical big data, and related areas from domestic and international universities, research institutions, biomedical engineering companies, and other organizations are cordially invited to participate in in-depth discussions, exchange ideas, and explore the latest research directions in the field of biomedical engineering, including organ-on-chips, organoids, and biomedical big data.

    The conference aims to showcase the latest scientific achievements, establish a platform for collaboration between research institutions and biomedical engineering companies, and collectively promote the development of organ-on-chip research and industry in China.

    The first five conferences have invited renowned experts in the field of organ-on-chip and tissue engineering, both internationally and domestically, including David Weitz, Dan Tagle, Kam Leong, Marcus Textor, Michael Shuler, Kevin Healy, Gordana Vunjak-Novakovic and Zhongze Gu.

    We sincerely welcome scholars, representatives from the business community, and graduate students engaged in biomedical engineering research to actively submit their papers and participate in the conference!
